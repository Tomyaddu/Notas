 ### Core Business Benefits

**Performance:** Eliminates thermal throttling for consistent 24/7 operation at maximum capacity (proven in El Capitan and Frontier supercomputers)

**Energy Efficiency:** Significantly reduces operational energy costs compared to traditional air cooling infrastructure

**Density Optimization:** Enables full rack population, reducing data center footprint requirements and improving connectivity

### Solution Selection Framework

**<10 kW:** Air cooling recommended

**10-20 kW:** Closed-loop solutions

**20-40 kW:** Liquid-to-air cooling

**40-70 kW:** Direct liquid cooling

**70-100+ kW:** Liquid cooling required

### HPE Competitive Differentiators

- **Rapid Deployment:** 8-month deployment cycle demonstrated (Bristol AI supercomputer)
- **Rack-and-Roll Solution:** Pre-integrated systems arrive tested and filled with coolant
- **Proven Technology:** 30+ years of experience with validated single-phase propylene glycol approach
- **Operational Efficiency:** Systems operate at 1MW+ with minimal acoustic footprint
- **Low Maintenance:** Annual coolant monitoring versus continuous air system maintenance
  
  ### Customer Value Messaging

**Asset Utilization:** Many data centers operate at only 30% rack capacity due to cooling constraints. Liquid cooling unlocks this stranded capital and defers facility expansion costs.

**Future Readiness:** Next-generation processors (CPUs >500W, GPUs >1000W) have reduced thermal tolerances that exceed air cooling capabilities.

**Thermal Management:** 100% heat capture ensures zero thermal impact on adjacent infrastructure, enabling mixed workload deployment.
